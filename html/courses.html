Pumpkin Carving Intensive

Tired of carving the lamest jack-o'-lanterns on the block? Tired of finishing third place in your local pumpkin carving contest year after year? Then this is the class for you. Pumpkin Carving Intensive provides you with the knowledge, insight, creativity, and steady hands to inscribe your gourd-like orange fruits with any ghoulish idea that pops into your head. In 24 short weeks, you'll be considered a virtuoso of traditional carving methods, as well as carving with stencils. Aside from carving, this course covers other necessary, and often overlooked, components, such as picking the right pumpkin, preparing the pumpkin for carving, and recycling or reusing the many byproducts of the pumpkin carving process. Pumpkin Painting Intensive

Similar to Pumpkin Carving Intensive, this course provides you with all tools necessary to etch masterpiece after masterpiece on to the uneven surfaces of these roughly spherical, orange fruits. As the course is only 36 weeks, you'll see yourself transform into the Beethoven of pumpkin painting in the blink of an eye. Many of our graduates have gone on to become pumpkin painting contest judges, adjunct professors of pumpkin painting, and full-time pumpkin painting bloggers. The History of Jack-o'-Lanters

Have you ever wondered why inconceivable amounts of people all across the world spend countless hours carving eye, nose, and mouth-shaped inscriptions into the sides of oddly-shaped and over-sized fruits during Halloween-time year after year? Well wonder no further. In this course, you'll perform a deep dive into the etymology and history of jack-o'-lanterns, as well as the pumpkin carving process itself. Our professors are widely considered as jack-o'-lantern pundits within the pumpkin carving community due to the incalculable amount of time and money spent researching, studying, and researching some more.
